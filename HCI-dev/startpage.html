Getting Started. Easily keep track of your assignments by creating an online tasklist in just a few steps.

Step 1:

Login to Canvas at https://lss.at.ufl.edu/

Step 2:

Navigate to "Settings" in the top-left corner of your dashboard.

Step 3:

Create a "New API Key" and copy your new token.

After syncing, view your assignments, add new tasks, and see "What's Next?" to work on.
